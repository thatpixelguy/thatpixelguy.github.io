Kid Tripp (iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch)

Kid Tripp is a tough-as-nails old-school platformer for iOS. (Emphasis on the "tough-as-nails." No, seriously. It's hard!)

I started writing the game from scratch during my senior year of highschool; after a couple years of working on it on and off, I released it on August 1, 2013. Critics and gamers generally dug it — you can read about what they have to say on the game's website and the App Store.
